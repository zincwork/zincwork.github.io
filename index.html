Fast decision making

With a straightforward integration, Zinc simplifies and accelerates the hiring journey. Gain access to full references on day one, before an interview has even taken place.

Real-time tracking

Watch references go from delivery to completion and automatically follow-up from those you haven't received.

Secure

Built on blockchain technology, Zinc creates a completely transparent and authenticated identity and work reputation system, eliminating many of the inefficiencies associated with hiring.

Empowering

Workers exercise complete control over how their data is shared, eliminating unqualified and unwelcome approaches.

Key features

Immutable references

Everlasting proof of your work history, dates and positions. Verified formal references, you can use over and over, backed-up by blockchain.

Aptitude & attitude reports

Find a img/team that you’ll thrive in first time around. Get statements from those who have long term insight into your work - reducing time from new joiner to high performer.

Interview proofs

Own previously inaccessible interview information. Zinc’s progressive platform allows you to hold proof of any tests or assessments collected whilst interviewing as interview proofs.

Anonymous hiring network

No spam, no time wasters. Interact in a network with proven information and no hiring bias. Get intelligently matched based skills and img/team fit then earn tokens by interacting.

Decentralised identity

Get a first of it’s kind, personal identity contract deployed on the decentralised web, you own and control. See in full transparency how information has been collected and who’s proofed it.

Data visualisations for social rankings

A ranking system for Github and Stackoverflow visualising how you rank against your peers for location and different languages.
