IDEX
Zinc tokens are redeemable for services on the Zinc platform. The Zinc token is a cryptoasset which is inherently volatile, hence there are no guarantees over the services which can be redeemed. Zinc tokens do not indicate a stake in the UK Ltd company - Zinc.

Token usages

Candidate referencing

Companies who wish to screen prospective workers can pay for the platform using ZINC.

Incentivized network

ZINC tokens are used to incentivize individuals for providing qualitative reference data to others via Zinc.

Decentralised identity

Zinc deploys ERC725 identity contracts for those that sign up to Zinc acting as your universal decentralised work ID.

Open community

Zinc has a discord chat forum, we hold votes to get community consensus on Zinc topics.
